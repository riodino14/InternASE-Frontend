OUR VISION

Creating an atmosphere and an environment that are comfortable in RPLGDC Laboratorium, creating a more quality assured working conditions & events, working together as well as expanding the sphere of influence to other IF Faculty laboratoriums and other majors in other faculties, giving a qualified & capable human resources output that are needed in the IT World.

OUR MISSION

  • Creating a clean, fresh, and also a comfortable laboratorium environment.
  • Giving more polished ‘quality-oriented’ laboratory work.
  • Making collaborations and workshops with other laboratories inside or outside of the IF Faculty.
  • Providing an opportunity to learn, propose possible projects, and implement brainstorming workshop sessions that could stimulate positive feedback in the IT World
  • Persuading other laboratories to achieve maximized performances through team.

  • Able to share many knowledges towards other people, inside or outside laboratorium environment.
  • Could implement all of the skills and knowledges that are available to development projects.

  • Having a place to better focus on research group that are always discussing about the latest topics going in software and/or game development world.
